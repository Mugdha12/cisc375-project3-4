Interesting facts about project

  • St. Anthony has less incident overall but when you only count theft related incident St. Anthony 23 has and Summit Hill has 16 incident which is the lowest.
  • During the school week of Oct 7-11 and during school time from 8am-3pm there noticebily less crime that is being commited, the highest being Capital River 59 and the lowest being Summit Hill with 4.
  • Between Aug. 14. 2014 to Oct.30 2019 there was been 5223 incident that were labled 'violent' crime. With St. Anthony being the lowest: 28 and with Payne/Phalen being the highest with 795.
  • There has been 2330 burglary incident types in 2014, whereas this count has decreased to 1909in 2018 suggesting a decrease of 18.1%.
  • The neighborhood Payne-Phalene recorded highest number of narcotics incident types for all the years. While, the neighborhood summit hill had the lowest number of narcotics incident types.
  • The highest number of arson, theft and weapon discharge incident types was 143, 6577, and 1393 was recorded in 2017 in all the neighborhoods. North end has the highest weapon discharge incident types, union park had the highest theft incidents and payne-phalene had the highest arson incident types. Overall, 2017 seem to have the highest number of incident types compared to the other years.
